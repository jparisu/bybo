Anatomy of a Brand

Every brand must have these five things. Here's each one — shown through ByBO itself.

Hover (or tap) a tile to see what each part means and why your brand needs it.

01

Name

ByBO
“Build Your Brand Online”

Name

Short, easy to say and memorable — it's the word people search for and tell their friends. “ByBO” is a snappy take on “Build Your Brand Online.”

02

Color Palette

Cyan · Navy · White

Color Palette

A few consistent colors set your mood and make you recognizable. Lead with one accent plus neutrals — ByBO pairs an electric cyan (#00d4ff) with a deep navy background.

03

Logo

Logo

One simple mark that reads at any size, from a browser favicon to a banner. Keep it geometric and uncluttered so it never turns to mush when small.

04

Typography & Font

Inter
Weights 300 – 800

Typography & Font

Type is your voice. One good family in a few weights keeps everything tidy. ByBO uses Inter — a clean, highly legible sans-serif — from light to extra-bold.

05

Style & Layout

Card grids & parallax

Style & Layout

Spacing, components and motion that repeat across every page tie a brand together. ByBO is dark-first with a light mode, glowing accents, card grids and gentle parallax.
